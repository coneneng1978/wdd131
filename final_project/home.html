EEVblog Tutorial

EEVblog, short for Electronics Engineering Video Blog, is a long-running YouTube channel created and hosted by Dave Jones, an Australian electronics engineer. Since its inception in 2009, the channel has gained a substantial following within the electronics engineering community and beyond.

The Foolish Engineer Tutorial

A YouTube channel created by an enthusiastic engineer who goes by the pseudonym of "The Foolish Engineer." The channel features a unique blend of humor, creativity, and informative content focused on engineering principles, projects, and real-world applications.

12VoltVids

A specialized YouTube channel that dives deep into the realm of 12-volt electrical systems used in automotive vehicles, boats, RVs, and related applications. The channel provides a blend of educational content, practical demonstrations, and insightful reviews aimed at enthusiasts, professionals, and DIYers alike.

Great Scott Tutorial

A popular YouTube channel hosted by Scott, known for his passion for electronics, DIY projects, and educational content related to technology. Scott offers detailed tutorials on fundamental electronics principles such as circuit design, component selection, soldering techniques, and troubleshooting. He also conducts experiments to demonstrate various electronic phenomena and principles.

ElectroBoom Tutorial

A highly popular and entertaining YouTube channel hosted by Mehdi Sadaghdar, known for its humorous take on electronics and engineering. It often features humorous yet informative videos where Mehdi demonstrates common mistakes in electronics, electrical safety precautions, and the consequences of improper handling of electronic components.

Engineering Mindset Tutorial

Engineering Mindset offers detailed tutorials and step-by-step guides on topics ranging from HVAC systems, electrical installations, plumbing, to structural engineering. These tutorials are designed for both students and professionals in the field.
